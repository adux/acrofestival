What's it all about

Welcome!
We'd like to invite you to the 2th Version of the Winter Acro Festival in Lenk, Switzerland. It's going to take place from:
22 till 27 of February 2019
With the experience of 2018 we made some upgrades!:
For starters it's going to be 6 days of Festival with 11 Rounds of Acro Workshops with handpicked teachers, but more about them later :). There are some additional Handstands, Yoga and Thai Massage classes planned ;). And since we're in the mountains, and it's winter, we'll add some Snow related activities. To close this year to relax - after hard training - we have Saunas you can enjoy in the evening.
