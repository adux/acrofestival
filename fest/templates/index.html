What's it all about

Welcome!
We'd like to invite you to the 2nd version of the Winter Acro Festival in Lenk, Switzerland. It's going to take place from:
22 till 27 of February 2019
With the experience of 2018 we made some upgrades: For starters we have 6 days of festival, 11 rounds of acro workshops and handpicked teachers, but more about them later :). There are some additional handstands, yoga and thai massage classes planned ;). Since we're in the mountains we'll add some snow activities. To close, this year - after hard training - we have saunas you can enjoy in the evening.

General Information

  • Accommodation

    This year the dorm is 4 min. walk from the hall - No LamaAirlines, sorry :( -. Important is that you bring your own sleeping bag! *Accommodation is not included in single day tickets. More important details:

    CLICK HERE

  • Food

    Food plays an important roll in this festival. Main dishes, healthy snacks and fruits are all bio, mostly local, vegan and cooked with love.
    * Please note that there will be some help asked from everyone arround small kitchen tasks.

    COOKING TEAM

  • Workshops

    There'll be 11 rounds of acro workshops and space for a constant jam. Experience in acro is important for your own enjoyment.
    * There will be no beginner workshops.

    TEACHERS

  • Special events

    On monday noon we organized a snow event up on the mountain at the ski resort (If the weather is good ). Bring warm cloths for out-door acro. Free afternoon for ski, snowboard or free floor at the hall.
    There's sauna to relax and an open stage to share your talents.
    *As an EXTRA, we could organise a day ski ticket for you.

    This Festival takes place in Lenk im Simmental, Switzerland. A complete description on how to get there can be found in the following link:
