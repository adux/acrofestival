What's it all about

{% tinycontent_simple 'home_intro'%} Welcome!
We'd like to invite you to the 2nd version of the Winter Acro Festival in Lenk, Switzerland. It's going to take place from:
22 till 27 of February 2019
With the experience of 2018 we made some upgrades: For starters we have 6 days of festival, 11 rounds of acro workshops and handpicked teachers, but more about them later :). There are some additional handstands, yoga and thai massage classes planned ;). Since we're in the mountains we'll add some snow activities. To close, this year - after hard training - we have saunas you can enjoy in the evening.

Teachers 2019

Celine & Alfonso - AcroWings

With these two beauties we go way back to the begginings of our carriers. Amazing friends and teachers!! We're very happy to have them back.
Alfonso has such a encouraging energy. You cannot stop him doing acro!
Celine, well... if her smilie doesn't captivate you, just wait to see her fly Icarians and whips. Jaw dropping.
They will be sharing their whips and Icarian moves in this Winter Festival 2019.

Trudi & Wybren

I'll be honest, first encounter was a bit intimidating. But then you go full FKK at some random acro conventions and you realise we're all in the same rail here (sort of).
It's endless what you could learn from this couple of amazing acrobats!
Wybren is teacher at the Codarts Circus School in Rotterdam and with Trudi they have given many of our favourite technical workshops. Very happy to have them on board!

Peter

We where on our way to give a workshop in Copenhagen and while stalking the local teachers we came across some pretty nice flows. Later that day we finally met the authors: Peter and Julie. So lovely and beautiful people!! and what a multi poli hiper mover Peter is. Very happy he accepted our invitation!

Fer & Fifi

We met this duo just recently at the 7th Barcelona Mano a Mano Convention and liked their way of teaching so much that we had to invite them.
Experts in acrobatics and many other Circus arts, they are going to share a piece of their knowledge with us! Thanks for accepting our invitation and happy to see you soon !

Paul

First time I read about Paul, they discribed him as the swiss knife of acrobatics. Soon enough we learned to know why: basing, flying and is basiclly a encyclopedia of creative tricks. He's coming to share hes many curious tricks and humor for the first time in the Swiss alps.

Daniel Marty

For being "just his hobby" Daniel has investes A LOT of time in acro, and another bunch in developing the Basel and Swiss acro community. Mostly traveling arround from one big event to another, he hardly misses the local events. Very happy to have him sharing all that collected knowleadge at the Winter Acro Festival this 2019!

Kerstin and Uwe - Cameo Acrobatix

It's been very nice to see how this friends have grown so fast and good in their Acro paths. Touring with their acro company all arround europe, giving very nice workshops. Very happy to have them on Board! We picked them up in Berlin and Graz to bring them direct to Lenk yuhuiii!

Bruno

I had to laugh only from remembering Bruno at the French acro convention, what a blast you'll have with this guy! Improvisation and staging are he's strengths and passion. But it's hard to find the words to describe such a clown, you just have to meet him. Happy to have you with us my friend!
