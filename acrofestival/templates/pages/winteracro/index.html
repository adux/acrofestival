Celine & Baptiste

Baptiste and Celine have been at the Winter Acro a few times. Remember all those one arm handstand workshops? or the super whips?. Legend says they found love at the Winter Acro... but thats a story for another time. Very happy to have them on board sharing all the super cool tricks.Technical, fun and creative what more could you ask for?

Eulalie

When jumping, flipping and turning is required on the trampolin Eulelie is the first on the line. For the first time in the Winter Acro she comes to share her passion. Thanks for being with us!

Bruno

I had to laugh only from remembering Bruno! Improvisation and staging are he's strengths and passion. But it's hard to find the words to describe such a clown, you just have to meet him. Happy to have you with us my friend!

Wybren is teacher at the Codarts Circus School in Rotterdam and with Trudi they have given many of our favourite workshops. Very happy to have them on board!

So lovely and beautiful people!! and what a multi poli hiper mover Peter is. Very happy he accepted our invitation!
