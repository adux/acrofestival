3rd. Urban Acro Festival - new edition!

This year it is going to be a special edition of the Urban Acro Festival. We would like to address acrobats and acroyoga lovers, who want to be part of this small acro gathering after a long time of social distancing. We have selected great teacher-friends around Switzerland and happy to share with you a cool mix of different challenging and fun acro workshops over this long festival weekend.

Acro and Training is our addiction but there is space for social, creative moments and stay tuned, we have a funny surprise!

We have this year only spots for 35 friends/participants.

Workshops

Teachers

Spreading acro is our goal. We are happy to offer you a nice workshop list with many different styles of movement:

Flowy, Balanced, Standing, Laying, Dynamic, Static, Intro and Advanced. It's three days of moving, sharing and having fun.

We have carefully chosen our teacher-team around the Switzerland Acro Community. We trust in them and love to offer you small, personalised workshops. Of conviction, we keep it small and familiarly. There will be also longe sessions with a coach. (advanced)

So all in all, we are looking forward to 6 Rounds with each 2 Workshops to choose from.

We'll list the Workshops as soon as possible, to receive the last news join or Newsletter or our Whatsapp Group

As a little extra, we have each day:

  • Morning Yoga Sessions
  • Partner Thai Massage
  • Handstand training
  • Jam with longe
What's included
  • main dishes and snacks, all vegan and organic
  • workshop from some awesome local teachers
  • all day jam to make sure you get enough acro
  • special saturday evening event ;-)

    • Food Corner

    Not to picky only local, organic and vegan with Love.

    We set a high value on healthy food. So everything you eat during this days comes from the local farmer and will be cooked with love. As we train all day long we need energy and we want to have hight quality.

    With this in mind, we look to each other, to our food and to all other living beings we share the planet with. Vegan, organic, regional, fair and food-waste-conscious ingredients and processes are a given, forming a solid foundation upon which creativity roams free.

    Sleeping Area

    Better together

    The gallery above the trainings hall and the barn in the Zirkusquartier area will be our two sleeping halls.

    Each spot fit approximately 25 people. Simple and easy first comes first serves.

    Bring your own sleeping bag and matt to sleep.

    More comfort?

    In Zurich City there are many hostels or check out www.airbnb.com

    BOS Schweiz

    together

    Acrolama is officially corporate partnership from BOSS Switzerland and support the one-tree-one-life campaign.

    We love it: the goal is simple, direct and they make it achievable.

    Through previous events we have planted 255 new seedlings. Now we ask YOU to help! At the registration desk will be a little donation box to support this campaign and safe our irreplaceable rain-forests. Thanks!
