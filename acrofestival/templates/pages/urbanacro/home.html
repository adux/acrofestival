About the urban acro festival in Zürich

Summer + Acro [Partner Acrobatics, Acroyoga, Acrodance] + Yoga + Handstand + Thai Massage = 3 days of tons of fun!

Züri Community

We want to share, play and laugh with all the Zürich Acro Community!!! There's probably no better place to spend this 3 days full of Acro, than the Zirkusquartier.You'll have more then 16 Acro Workshops, daily yoga and handstand classes, awesome food and more then one special surpirse during the Weekend!

The invitation is open for ALL levels, experiences, shapes and sizes! EVERYONE is welcome to come and share the fun of Acro and move with us!

Workshops

We loved last years fest with mainly Züri Teachers it really gave a full overview of the local scene. This year we want to include teachers form all over Switzerland. Flowy, Balanced, Standing, Laying, Dynamic, Static, Intro and Advanced. It's 3 days of moving, sharing and having fun.

There will be 8 Rounds with each 2 Workshops to Choose from. We'll list the Workshops as soon as posible, to recive the last news join or Newsletter or our Whatsapp Group

As a little extra, we have each day:

  • Morning Yoga Sessions
  • Partner Thai Massage
  • Handstand training
  • Jam with longe
Not only Acro!

Many more things then just a floor to do acro and a teacher are included in this weekend!

  • Awesome local, vegan and organic food
  • Live Band and DJ for the Saturday Party
  • ...lets play misterious ... we have some surprise!

Food Corner

Not to picky only local, fair, organic and vegan with Love.

We set a high value on healthy food. So everything you eat during this days comes from the local farmer and will be cooked with love. As we train all day long we need energy and we want to have hight quality.

With this in mind, we look to each other, to our food and to all other living beings we share the planet with. Vegan, organic, regional, fair and food-waste-conscious ingredients and processes are a given, forming a solid foundation upon which creativity roams free.

Kremena our cook is insatiably curious about all aspects of plant-based cuisine, constantly nibbling on fascinating information about preparation, nutrition, origin and sustainability. She particularly enjoys sharing food.

As she cooked allready amazing food for our last Winter Acro Festival, she has this experience and know who much you Acro-Monkey can eat! ;-)

Sleeping Area

Better together

The gallery above the trainings hall and the barn in the Zirkusquartier area will be our two sleeping halls.

Each spot fit approximately 25 people. Simple and easy first comes first serves.

Bring your own sleeping bag and matt to sleep.

More comfort?

In Zurich City there are many hostels or check out www.airbnb.com

Zürich roots ;D

Feet on the head! Awesome acrobats, who love to move and know many may tricks in standing. We are happy to have them at the Urban Acro Festival again!

Salvatore & Laura

Zürich

He teaches Yoga, AcroYoga, Meditation and Slackline and combines these activities in a creative and playful way. The practice of extreme sports combined to meditation establishes the balance between playing and staying. In both activities the ultimate goal is to live here and now, to play here and now, to stay here and now.

Michael

Michaels first passion was Tango, Argentinan dance he teaches for many years in St. Gallen, Winthertur and Zürich. With this backgroup, you can expect Accents & Shapings to be on the vocabulary of his performing Worshops!

Lorenz & Cornelia

Zürich

Much could be said about the Duo Scacciapensieri, they are by far the most exprienced teachers coming this year. Colorfull performances are always in the pallete. They have their own Kid Zirkus School, a Handstand Academy and are founders of Manege at the ASVZ.

Tanja & Lisa

Zürich

We are two women passionately practicing AcroYoga for around 5 years. It’s important for us to create an environment in which everyone can immerse him/herself into all the roles of AcroYoga. We invite you to challenge your mental boundaries of “I can’t do that, I am too weak”, to fly and trust. Immerse yourself with us into learning some beautiful poses, fun flows and engaging in a mindful group practice!
